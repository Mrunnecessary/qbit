Qbit — Investor Overview

Tagline: AI that thinks with you

Problem

AI tools today provide answers, but users struggle with:

  • Lack of clarity and structure
  • Information overload
  • No way to reuse or organize AI outputs

Solution

Qbit is a transparent AI assistant and research workspace designed to help users understand, decide, and organize information in daily life, work, and learning.

Product Vision

Qbit aims to become a daily-use AI companion that combines:

  • Transparent AI search & summaries
  • Decision-oriented explanations
  • Pinning & project-based organization
  • Productivity outputs like documents and presentations

Target Users

  • Students & exam aspirants
  • Professionals & job seekers
  • Founders, researchers & small businesses

Business Model

Qbit follows a scalable SaaS model with:

  • Consumer subscriptions
  • Premium productivity features
  • Enterprise and B2B solutions

Detailed monetization strategies and financials are shared privately with interested investors.

Current Status

  • MVP live (web)
  • Early user waitlist active
  • Founder-led development
