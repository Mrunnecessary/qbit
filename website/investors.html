Qbit – Investor Overview

Vision

Build the career intelligence layer for the AI era.

Problem

Millions of professionals struggle to understand:

  • Which skills they already have
  • Which skills they actually need
  • Whether they are ready for a new career role

Most online courses and career advice platforms provide generic guidance without measuring skill readiness.

Solution

Qbit analyzes user skills and calculates career alignment scores for future roles.

The platform highlights:

  • Skill readiness
  • Skill gaps
  • Learning roadmap
  • Relevant job opportunities

Current Status

  • Early waitlist users
  • Founder-led product development
  • Career intelligence MVP launching

Long-Term Vision

Qbit aims to evolve into a career operating system that helps individuals continuously measure and improve their professional readiness.

Future Platform Expansion

Future versions of Qbit will include recruiter-facing tools that allow companies to discover candidates based on verified skill alignment scores rather than traditional resumes.
