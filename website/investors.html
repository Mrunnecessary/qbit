Investor Overview

Building the career intelligence layer for the AI era.

The Problem

Millions of professionals want to switch careers but don't know where to start. Existing platforms are static and resume-based — they tell you what you've done, not what you're capable of next.

Nobody tells professionals: "Here's exactly how ready you are for your target role, and here's what to do about it."

The Solution

Qbit is an AI-powered Career Intelligence Engine. Users describe their daily work — Qbit detects transferable skills, calculates a readiness score for their target role, identifies skill gaps, and generates a personalized growth roadmap.

No resume required. Results in 30 seconds. Actionable from day one.

Defensible Moat

Long-Term Vision

Qbit evolves into a Career Operating System — continuously measuring and improving professional readiness. The end state: every professional has a live career readiness score, updated as their skills grow and job markets shift.

Technology

  • Backend: Google Apps Script (scaling to Node.js/FastAPI)
  • AI: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for skill extraction and career intelligence
  • Database: Google Sheets (scaling to PostgreSQL)
  • Email: Resend for transactional and follow-up automation
