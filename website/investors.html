Qbit — Investor Overview

Tagline: AI that shows its thinking

Problem

As AI becomes more capable, users increasingly struggle to trust its outputs.

  • AI answers hide assumptions and uncertainty
  • Conflicting information is collapsed into one response
  • Users cannot understand why an answer was given
  • AI outputs are disposable and cannot be reused meaningfully

Insight

The next challenge in AI is not generating better answers — it is helping humans understand and decide with confidence.

Solution

Qbit is a reasoning-first AI system designed to make thinking transparent.

Instead of rushing to answers, Qbit:

  • Makes assumptions explicit
  • Separates known facts from unknowns
  • Shows multiple valid perspectives
  • Explains reasoning step by step
  • Keeps humans in control of final decisions

Product Vision

Qbit is building the thinking layer for the AI era.

As AI models and agents become more powerful, Qbit sits upstream — helping users reason clearly before actions are taken.

The product is intentionally model-agnostic and designed to remain relevant as the AI ecosystem evolves.

Target Users (Initial Focus)

  • Knowledge workers making non-trivial decisions
  • Founders and professionals evaluating trade-offs
  • Users who value clarity over speed

Business Model

Qbit follows a capital-efficient SaaS model:

  • Individual subscriptions for advanced reasoning features
  • Professional plans for reuse and decision history

Low infrastructure costs allow profitability with a relatively small paid user base.

Defensibility Strategy

  • Structured decision frameworks
  • User feedback learning loop
  • Retention-driven product design
  • Positioning as clarity layer in AI ecosystem

Early Traction

  • 20 users on early-access waitlist
  • Direct founder-user feedback loop active
  • Retention-focused MVP in progress
