Frequently Asked Questions

What is Qbit?

Qbit is an AI-powered career intelligence platform that analyzes your daily work activities to detect transferable skills and estimate your readiness for different career roles.

How does Qbit calculate career readiness?

Qbit detects skills from your work description and compares them with the required skills of multiple professional roles. The score is calculated based on how many required skills are matched.

Do I need to upload my resume?

No. You can simply describe your daily work activities. Resume upload is optional.

Does Qbit store my resume?

No. If you upload a resume, it is only analyzed temporarily to detect skills and is not stored.

Why does Qbit ask for my email?

Your email helps us improve career recommendations and send optional updates about new career insights and tools.

Is Qbit free?

The career readiness analysis is free. Advanced career roadmap tools and coaching features may be introduced in future versions.
