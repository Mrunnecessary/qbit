Frequently Asked Questions

What is Qbit?

Qbit is a career intelligence platform that analyzes your current skills and shows how prepared you are for future roles such as Data Analyst, Backend Developer, or Cloud Engineer.

How is Qbit different from other AI tools?

Most AI tools give generic advice like “learn Python” or “learn SQL”. Qbit measures your existing skills and calculates your readiness before suggesting what to learn next.

Who should use Qbit?

Qbit is designed for:

  • Professionals switching careers
  • People exploring technology roles
  • Students choosing career paths
  • Anyone confused by online courses and career advice

How accurate are Qbit scores?

Scores are indicative estimates based on the information provided by the user and publicly available skill requirements for different roles. They are intended to guide learning priorities rather than replace professional hiring evaluations.

Does Qbit store my work description?

User descriptions may be temporarily processed to generate skill analysis. Qbit does not publish or share personal inputs publicly.

Does Qbit apply to jobs for me?

No. Qbit helps you understand your readiness, identify skill gaps, and find relevant job opportunities with official links.

Is Qbit free?

Qbit is currently in early access. Early users can explore the product and help shape future features.
