Frequently Asked Questions

What is Qbit?

Qbit is a decision intelligence platform that makes AI reasoning transparent and structured.

How is it different?

Qbit focuses on clarity. It highlights assumptions, uncertainty, and multiple viewpoints instead of giving only fast answers.

Who is it for?

Professionals, founders, learners, and anyone making thoughtful decisions.

Does Qbit execute actions?

No. Qbit is intentionally human-in-the-loop.

Is it free?

Currently in early access.
